Frequently Asked Questions

How do I store my biltong?

▼

Keep biltong in a cool, dry place. Once opened, store in a breathable paper bag and consume within a week. Avoid sealed plastic in warm conditions to prevent moisture build-up.

Is your biltong gluten-free?

▼

Yes. Our spice blends are gluten-free and contain no MSG. Please see each product page for full ingredients.

When will my order arrive?

▼
